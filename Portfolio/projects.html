My Projects

Snowy Ice Treats

Snowy’s Ice Treats is a new pet treat brand that focuses on creating delicious and unique ice cream treats for dogs! They have a varying amount of products, ranging from standard ice cream flavors, ice cream treat minis, to even an ice cream treat subscription service. All of these products come with their own unique packaging which reflects the brand and its flavors.

20th Annual Orchid Blues Festival

The annual orchid blues festival is an event that happens every year in order to celebrate local blues musicians in NC. In order to promote the event, posters, and banners, as well as an event program was created. The pieces are designed to reflect the event’s name as well as the blues theme.

Funky Freaks Trading Card Game

Funky Freaks is a brand-new trading card game developed by Dorado toys. It’s similar to popular trading card games of the past but more geared toward a younger audience. The design of the packaging and the cards themselves are meant to reflect the more childlike and funky theme of the card game.

Cape Lookout NC Travel Campaign

Cape Lookout is a beautiful location in Nc that is looking to attract more tourists to its area. To accomplish this a series of advertisements, illustrations, and gift shop goods are created in order to rebrand the area and create an image that is appealing to visitors.

Atlas Sporting Goods

An advertisement campaign for Atlas Sporting Goods, consisting of social media ads, billboards, and website banners, with the purpose of rebranding their image, in order to appeal to a younger audience and increase sales.

Ditko Poetry Classic

A set of book cover designs, as well as additional promotional materials created for the well-established publishing company Ditko poetry classics.

Psychology Today Magazine Illustration and Layout

Illustrated cover, spread, and layout designed for the magazine Psychology Today. It is made to reflect the contents of the lead article titled “Sooner or later we all face death. Will a sense of meaning help us?” written by Warren Wardis, edited by Nigel Warburton.

Icarus wine

Icarus wine is a luxury wine brand that originated in Spain. It produces a variety of different high-quality wines, which are delivered to its customer in bottles enwrapped with uniquely illustrated art that depicts the story of Icarus. In addition, these bottles are packaged within a beautifully engraved wooden encasement, ensuring a positive buyer experience.
